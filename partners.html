Partners & Associates

SC Insurance Advisors is proud to have the following individuals to provide our clients with the best possible insurance solutions.

Randy Perko, President

Randy founded SCIA 3 years ago after a 12 year career with Humana as a leading sales executive, 5 years as a Financial Service Representative with New York Life, 3 years as a Life and Annuity Specialist with TIAA, and 5 years in various roles with Sentry Insurance. Randy enjoys serving on the board of directors for Firefly Autism, golf, travel, hunting and fishing, time with his wife, Jessen, and their two dogs.

Jeff Spiers, VP & Director of Group Benefits

Jeff Spiers is the President and Founder of SpiersBenefits, based in Denver, Colorado. Since founding the company in June 2011, he has been providing health and welfare plans across over 25 states, focusing on services such as ICHRA, ACA, and Medicare. Previously, Jeff held significant roles at Change Healthcare, Vertafore, and Vizient, Inc., where he directed various insurance services and benefits consulting operations. Jeff is a family man at heart. He loves to spend time with his children and grandchildren, and he is an avid golfer.

Ray Ruiz, Advisor

Ray Ruiz has been with SCIA for 1 year, assisting and servicing clients with Medicare, Ancillary, Life, Annuity, and Care products. Ray joined SCIA from a career at TIAA as a Senior Management Specialist. Ray enjoys bowling, golfing, and enjoying time with his two dogs.

Charmaine Camvel, Advisor

Charmaine Camvel has been with SCIA for nearly a year, assisting and servicing clients with Medicare, Ancillary, Life, Annuity, and Care products. Charmaine joined SCIA from a 15 year career with Humana as an internal sales assistant. Charmaine enjoys teaching yoga, hiking, and time with her cats.

Brandi Dixon, Advisor

Brandi Dixon has been with SCIA for 2 years assisting in clients in customer service and experience. She is currently working on her insurance licensing to progress to a sales associate. Brandi joined SCIA from a long career in beauty services. Brandi enjoys cooking, being a mother of two and spending time with her two dogs.
